Nail Polish Party

WiCS hosted its third, bi-annual Nail Polish Party last D Term - a way for women all over WPI to get together and paint each others nails, play games and relax before finals! This year, WiCS will be hosting two more Nail Polish Parties, one in A Term and D term. Be on the look out for locations this year!

Outreach - Video Games Programming

WiCS members members taught video game programming to local middle school girls in a nine-week after school program run by Girls Inc.
